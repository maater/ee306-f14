10/6/14

Exam 1 Information

Exam 1 will be during class time (5:00 - 6:30 PM) this Wednesday, October 8. We recommend you arrive at least 5 minutes early so that you have plenty of time to take the exam. Please read the following guidelines carefully before Wednesday:
  • You will have 75 minutes to take the exam.
  • We will project the current time at the front of the room.
  • There should be NO writing when time is called. Anyone caught writing after time is called will be given a 0.
  • You may only keep a pencil and your cheat sheets on your desk. All other belongings must be placed at the front of the room.
  • You are allowed 3 sheets of notes, front and back, of any size on which you may write whatever you like. These notes must be in your own handwriting.
  • BRING YOUR STUDENT ID. We will verify your identity before entering the examination room and after we collect your exam.
  • Failure to follow directions will result in a 0. No exceptions.
  • You must sit with at least one empty seat between you and each of your neighbors.
To ensure that there is enough space for the exam, we will be splitting the students into 3 rooms. The rooms are assigned by last name as follows:
  • ETC 2.108 (Our classroom): Last names from A - L
  • JGB 2.202: Last names from M - Ramos
  • NOA 1.116: Last names from Raterink - Z
