Policy

As discussed in class, there are two cases for the regrade:

  1. If you make minor changes (e.g., adding/fixing an instruction to store the result in x3101), you must send an email explaining what you fixed as specified in the instructions below. If we agree that what you fixed was minor, you can get up to 90 points.
  2. If you make significant changes, (e.g. you rewrite the whole program), you should not send any emails. You will get the average of the new grade and the original grade.

Instructions

  • Fix your program: Fix your program and name the file containing your program reverse.regrade.bin.

  • Submit your program: Submit your program in your Project 1 Directory. Follow the standard submission instructions page to do so. Start at the "Updating your Submission Directory" Step. You should see an empty "reverse.regrade.bin" file. Be sure to verify your submission using your internet browser.

  • If you made major changes to your program, you are done and no further action is required. However, if you fixed a minor error then you should read on.

  • Email Comments: If and only if you made a minor fix, please send an email to faruk@hps.utexas.edu. The email should follow the following template:
    Subject: EE306 regrade for your UT EID
    Message body: describe what you fixed (in less than 30 words)

NOTES

  1. Do not email if you rewrote your program or changed it substantially.

  2. A minor change regrade applies to an absolute maximum of two lines of code.

  3. If you decide to email, please adhere to the above guidelines. Emails not following it will be ignored.

  4. Be sure your submission is done by the deadline, November 6, 11:59pm.
